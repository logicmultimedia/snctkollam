Post graduate Courses
M.Com Finance : (Course Code: 590)

The duration of the course is four semesters (2 years), Number of seats - 10 50% seats are allotted by the University on the basis of online applications registered in the University website: www.admissions.keralauniversity.ac.in

The remaining 50% seats are filled by the Management. Applicants seeking Management seat should also register on-line at the University Website.

This career-related first degree programme in Master of Commerce (Finance) aims at inculcating essential skills as demanded by the global soft-ware industry through interactive learning process. The course is designed to develop computer professionals versatile in the use of computers in almost all fields of Computer Application. This course serves to take up jobs such as software engineer, software consultant, network engineer, database administrator, system analyst, programmer etc. (This course description is about BCA, please change it)

Eligibility

A Pass in CBCS Pattern

Commerce as core course with not less than 1.8 CGPA (s) out of 4 in B.Com / B.Com. Computer Application /B.Com Tourism and Travel Management (Career Related / Vocational / Restructured) B.Com. Actuarial Science (Vocational ) B.Com. Office Management & Secretarial Practice / B.Com. Hotel Management & Catering (Career Related / Restructured) BBA / BBS / BBM.

  • In the case of marks and gradesystem (indirect grading system) minimum marks /SCPA will be as per the University Rules.
